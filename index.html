EquipShare

EquipShare allows users to share items in their communities. Users can sign up and see items they own and the people who want to rent them.

Technologies: Rails, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Github.

Gotta Cache'em all

Gotta Cache'em all is a game to allow users to go around a city and catch different Pokemon using QR codes that have individual Pokemon. Users can sign in and see a list of events, where Pokemon can be caught using google maps api.

Technologies: Rails, HTML, CSS/SaSS, Bootstrap, Github.

Vagabond

Vagabond allows a user to sign up/login, blog and read posts according to the city that they are in. Full CRUD on posts as well as authorization and authentication restrictions.

Technologies: Rails, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Github.

Workout Tracker

Workout Tracker allows you create and delete workouts and refer to them for later use. Developed an app that connects to a database, allows you to add a workout, view and click into that specific workout to see more information. It also allows you to delete a specific workout.

Technologies: Javascript, HTML, CSS, AJAX, Bootstrap, Node.js, Mongo, Express, Github.
